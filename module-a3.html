What is Personal Injury Law?

The Foundation: Helping Injured People

🎯 GLG's Mission: We represent people who have been hurt through no fault of their own. We fight for fair compensation so our clients can rebuild their lives.

Personal injury law exists because accidents happen — and when they do, someone is usually at fault. Our job is to prove who was negligent and get our clients the money they need to move forward.

GLG training: Problem-Solving, Not Just Law

🧠 Key Insight: "Excellent lawyering is built on interviewing, counseling, negotiation, and persuasive fact analysis — not just knowing the law." Every GLG team member uses these skills.

You don't have to be an attorney to apply these principles:

  • Interviewing: Learning the client's real situation and concerns
  • Counseling: Explaining options clearly and helping clients decide
  • Negotiation: Understanding what motivates the other side
  • Fact Analysis: Building a story that shows why our client deserves compensation

The Four Elements of Negligence

Every personal injury case requires proving four things:

  1. Duty: The defendant owed a duty to be careful
  2. Breach: They violated that duty
  3. Causation: Their violation caused the accident
  4. Damages: The plaintiff suffered real harm

If any element is missing, there's no case. All four must be proven for a successful outcome.

The Client-Focused Approach

❤️ Remember: Every case decision starts with "how does this serve the client?" The legal result AND the client experience both matter.

PI clients are scared, hurt, and dealing with insurance companies for the first time. They need an attorney who fights for them AND treats them with empathy throughout the process.

GLG Practice Areas

Cases We Handle

GLG focuses on personal injury cases where someone was hurt through no fault of their own. Here are our primary practice areas with the PI strategy safety rule framework:

🚗 Motor Vehicle Accidents

Car crashes, rear-end collisions, intersection accidents
Safety Rule: Every driver must follow traffic laws and drive with reasonable care

🚛 Commercial Trucking

18-wheeler crashes, delivery truck accidents, commercial vehicle collisions
Safety Rule: Commercial drivers must follow federal safety regulations

🏍️ Motorcycle Accidents

Bike vs. car collisions, lane splitting incidents, visibility-related crashes
Safety Rule: All drivers must watch for motorcycles and share the road safely

🚶 Slip and Fall

Wet floors, uneven surfaces, inadequate lighting, debris
Safety Rule: Property owners must maintain safe conditions for visitors

⚕️ Medical Malpractice

Misdiagnosis, surgical errors, medication mistakes, delayed treatment
Safety Rule: Healthcare providers must follow accepted standards of care

🐕 Dog Bite

Attacks, bites, knock-downs, inadequate restraint
Safety Rule: Dog owners must control their animals to prevent harm to others

💀 Wrongful Death

Fatal accidents, medical negligence, workplace incidents
Safety Rule: Same as underlying case — defendant's violation caused a death

🚗 Uber/Lyft Accidents

Rideshare crashes, driver negligence, passenger injuries
Safety Rule: Rideshare drivers must drive safely and follow company policies

Case Evaluation Using GLG training

🔍 The Four Challenges in Case Counseling: (1) Create options beyond obvious, (2) Work out each precisely, (3) Explain clearly, (4) Maintain professional empathy

When discussing cases with clients, we don't just say "we'll get you money." We explain the options (settle vs. trial), work out the numbers (medical bills, lost wages, pain/suffering), and help them make informed decisions.
