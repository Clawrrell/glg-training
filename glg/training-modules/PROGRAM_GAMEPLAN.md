## PROGRAM STRUCTURE (PROPOSED)

### TIER 1: UNIVERSAL (Everyone takes this, new + existing)
- Module 1: GLG Operations — How We Work
- Module 2: Communication Standards & CasePeer
- Module 3: Legal Compliance & Ethics
- Module 4: Government Claims
- Monthly Quiz Competition

### TIER 2: NEW HIRE ONBOARDING (4-week structured program)
Week 1 — Foundations:
- Day 1: GLG Operations (Module 1) + PI Basics
- Day 2: PI Case Types, Liability, CasePeer Statuses
- Day 3: Insurance (BI/PD/UM/UIM)
- Day 4: Key Documents (LORs, LOPs, Police Reports, Templates)
- Day 5: Week 1 Review & Assessment

Week 2 — Core Tasks:
- Day 6: Medical Records (requesting, following up, HIPAA)
- Day 7: Coverage Verification & Opening Claims
- Day 8: Lien Basics (what they are, who gets paid)
- Day 9: Client Communication (scripts, logging, 14-day rule)
- Day 10: Week 2 Review

Week 3 — Department Specific (role-based):
- CM Track: Demand prep, adjuster negotiation, settlement calls
- LA Track: Records management, task tracking, escalation
- Intake Track: Screening, conflict checks, government entity ID
- Lit Track: Litigation process, discovery, service of process

Week 4 — Integration:
- Shadow assigned CM/attorney
- Handle tasks independently with supervision
- 30-day competency assessment
- Feedback and sign-off

### TIER 3: CURRENT EMPLOYEE PROGRAM
- All staff take Tier 1 modules (even veterans)
- Department-specific modules assigned based on role
- Monthly quiz competition ($100 gift card)
- Scorecard tracks training completion

### TIER 4: LEADERSHIP (Adriauna, Landon, Firm Admin)
- Attorney Supervision & Ethics (Rules 5.1/5.3)
- Scorecard Delivery & Feedback
- Case Review & Quality Control
- Staff Development & Coaching
- Client Escalation Management

## GAMIFICATION SYSTEM
- Points per module completed (10 pts)
- Bonus for 100% quiz score (5 pts)
- "Level Up" progression:
  - Level 1: Trainee (0-30 pts)
  - Level 2: Associate (31-60 pts)
  - Level 3: Specialist (61-90 pts)
  - Level 4: Expert (91+ pts)
- Monthly quiz winner: $100 gift card
- Quarterly top scorer: half day off or $250
- Leaderboard in Google Sheet (already built)

## 30/60/90 DAY CHECKPOINTS
- Day 30: Can employee handle core tasks independently?
- Day 60: Is employee meeting scorecard standards?
- Day 90: Full performance review, training completion verified
